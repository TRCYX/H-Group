---
title: Glossary
---

This is a glossary of common terms that have specific meaning in the H-Group convention framework.

| Term               | Definition                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            |
| ------------------ | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| _Clued_            | A property of a card. A card is considered _Clued_ if it has been given one or more positive clues from other players. A card remains clued until it is either played or discarded. A card being _Clued_ is the same thing as a card being _Touched_. |
| _Dupe_             | A property of a card. Short for _Duplicate_. A card is considered a _Dupe_ if two or more copies of the card are currently _Clued_.                                                                                                                   |
| _Finesse Position_ | See the [_Finesse Position_ section](level-1.mdx#finesse-position) in level 1.                                                                                                                                                                        |
| _Playable_         | A property of a card. See the [Definition of Playable section](level-1.mdx#the-definition-of-playable) in level 1.                                                                                                                                    |
| _Touched_          | A property of a card. See the definition for _Clued_.                                                                                                                                                                                                 |
| _Trash_            | A property of a card. A card is considered _Trash_ if it can never play on the stacks or it is a _Dupe_.                                                                                                                                              |
